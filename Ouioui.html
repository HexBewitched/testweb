Ouioui

"These juvenile Peepys have arrived just on the precipice of self awareness. So far, they know two things- Eating peanuts and sleeping. Aren't they cute? Whatever."

Ouiouis are younger Peepys, ones that haven't matured enough to become a Peepy. They come in mystery blind bags, with there currently having been two blind bag series, having a total of 12 Ouioui variations across the two series. Currently, series one has ceased production, while series two is still being sold.









Series 1

Ouioui Series 1 was the first series of Ouioui blind bags sold on ItemLabel.com. It included five Ouioui variations, Cow, Stinker, Lemon, Sunny Day, and Gold. According to ItemLabel, there was a 10 in 41 chance of getting Cow, Stinker, Lemon, or Sunny Day, and a 1 in 41 chance of getting Gold.
Every Ouioui has a stout body, with one orb constituting it. Ouiouis have only two feet, and their antennae sits between their ears. Series one is currently out of production.









Series 2

Ouioui Series 2 is the second Ouioui blind bag series sold on ItemLabel.com. It includes seven Ouioui variations, two more than the previous series. Beeoui, Fertilizer, Purple Fuzz, Tomato, and Strawberry are common Ouiouis, with each one having a 19.4% chance of being in one bag. Silver, the rare Ouioui, has a 2.8% chance of being in a bag, and the Secret Rare Ouioui has a 0.28% chance of being in a bag.
The Ouioui Series 2 blindbags are currently available, and are on sale for $15.00 on ItemLabel.com
