Peepy

Peepy is a collectible item made by the company ItemLabel. It is sold completely online, and has the appearance of a beaked animal with bulbous spheres for a body and feet. Peepys have many variations, with there being 17 documented as of this moment. There are also Ouiouis, which are younger Peepys that have only one sphere for a body.
The Cow Peepy, a.k.a. Cowpy (pictured left) was the first one, and is believed to be the common ancestor of all other Peepys. They are sold for $30.00 on ItemLabel.com
















Ouioui

Ouioui is a collectible item made by the company ItemLabel. It is sold completely online, and shares a physical appearance with the Peepy. Ouiouis are actually young Peepys, with their biggest distinction being their bodies, as Ouiouis have only one sphere as a body, while Peepys have two. Ouiouis also have only two legs, while Peepys have four. There are 12 documented Ouioui variations, some of which resemble their adult counterparts.
Ouiouis are sold in blind-bags, unlike their older counterpart, and are currently on their second series. They are sold for $17.00 on ItemLabel.com
