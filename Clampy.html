Yellow Clam Peeoui (Clampy)

"So this OuiOui is kind of a slacker and hasn't really left the house for a while. They need to get a job and start saving up but they can't find one anywhere. They still throw tantrums all the time, collect trading cards, and play gacha games. I'm sick of this crap."

Clampy is a rare variation of Peepy AND Ouioui. It is a Peeoui, which is the point where a Ouioui begins to develop into a Peepy. It has a Peepy's beak and face, but is still the size of a Ouioui. The slacker has the body of a clam, with its beak acting like the clam's hinge. Its lemon-yellow color covers their entire body. Somehow, this Peeoui has no legs. It has been doomed to wander this world missing body parts. Existence is truly painful for Peepys, isn't it?
Clampy is sold on ItemLabel.com for $20.00.
