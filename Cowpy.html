Cow Peepy (Cowpy)

STRENGTH: GRASS EATING
WISDOM: ACCOUNTING
PSYCHIC: MISSING TIME
WEAKNESS: PROBE
BIRTHDAY: JUNE 29

The Cowpy was the first Peepy ever made. It's about 6 inches from front to back, and has glassy eyes. This Peepy has the appearance of a cow, and has small nubs for feet. Cowpy is the unofficial mascot of the Peepy merchandise line, as it has appeared in the most advertisements, and is the most famous Peepy variation. This Peepy is also accompanied by Mega Peepy, which is a bigger version of Cowpy. It is sold for $30.00 on ItemLabel.com
