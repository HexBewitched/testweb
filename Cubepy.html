Green Cube Peepy (Cubepy)

STRENGTH: MINING
WISDOM: TORCH
PSYCHIC: DIAMOND
WEAKNESS: TNT
BIRTHDAY: DECEMBER 30

Cubepy is a cuboid variation of a Peepy, if you couldn't tell. Its body is lime-green, with a white tail and brown feet. The most obvious characteristic of Cubepy is its body shape, as it's the only other Peepy with a cuboid body, alongside Thicc Brungus. The little guy is an avid gamer, and his design is based on the remarkably underrated and unknown game Minecraft. You should seriously check it out, it's underrated.
