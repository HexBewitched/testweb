Axolotl Peepy (Axolotlpy)

STRENGTH: WATER
WISDOM: FRIDGE
PSYCHIC: EMPATH
WEAKNESS: INTELLIGENCE
BIRTHDAY: DECEMBER 1

Axolotlpy is one of the many variants of the Peepy. Its ears are replaced with fin-like accessories, and its body is hot pink. Their tail also differs from the others, as it is an axolotl fin.
This Peepy mirrors the real life axolotl, a fish found in Mexico that is known for its cute appearance. This Peepy, however, has replaced the axolotl's face with its own, its dead stare always seeking you.
